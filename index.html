Making a Pizza

  1. Prepare Dough: Mix flour, yeast, salt, water, and olive oil. Knead until smooth, then let it rise for 1-2 hours.
  2. Preheat Oven: Set to 475°F (245°C).
  3. Roll Dough: Flatten dough on a floured surface to desired thickness.
  4. Add Sauce: Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Add Toppings: Sprinkle with cheese and add desired toppings (e.g., pepperoni, vegetables).
  6. Bake: Place pizza on a baking sheet or pizza stone and bake for 10-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  7. Serve: Let it cool slightly, slice, and enjoy!
